elixir

communication for nutrition.

The Elixir App allows grocery stores and food banks to communicate through a shared database in order to reduce the amount of food waste produced by stores.

sign-in

As a food bank, create an online account.

view

Check out the list of participating grocery stores and sort through the food they are willing to donate, the pick up time, and the distance.

request

If there is any food of interest, send a message to the grocery store requesting the food.
